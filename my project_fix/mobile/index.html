Introducing Indonesia’s newest reference in luxury hospitality, The Trans Luxury Hotel. A creation by Trans Corpora, it is a sophisticated blend of Indonesian tradition with a refined contemporary interpretation, right in the heart of the city of Bandung.

The Trans Luxury Hotel

All the facilities and services at The Trans Luxury Hotel have been designed to set the property in a class of its own. The panoramic restaurant and lounge on level 18 with sweeping views of the city, the open-air sandy beach pool with sun lounges, the elegant day spa, the high tech fitness center, the luxurious room amenities, all ensure the most memorable stay at a successful, impeccably run event.

Luxury Hotel in The Integrated Trans Studio Bandung Complex

Opened in 2012, and conveniently located in close proximity to Bandung’s Central Business District, this iconic 18-storey hotel with 280 guest rooms and suites is situated in the heart of The Integrated Trans Studio Complex Bandung, with direct access to the convention centre, shopping mall and theme park.
