MICE & Celebrations

Trans Grand Ballroom

Trans Grand Ballroom (TGB) with its palatial pre-function area is ideal for impressive events, memorable weddings and spectacular parties with its high ceilings, breathtaking adornments, glowing chandeliers and floral motif carpeting, The Grand Ballroom adds just the right glint of allure and grandeur to your next event.

Trans Boardroom

Our multipurpose boardrooms function space centered in mezzanine level that each room can accommodate up-to 35 guests and are ideal for private executive meetings or private conferences.

Trans Convention Center

Is the largest premium meeting, conference and exhibition venue in Bandung. Provides the perfect setting for up to 6,000 persons as well as offering its guests the ideal place in which to turn their wildest ideas into reality, located on the 3rd floor with convenient access within the Trans Studio Mall.

The Jewel Box

The Jewel Box is a plush VIP room with customized appeal, glamour and specially designed to complement the most exclusive private events and parties. This intimate setting VIP room can accommodate up to 60 guests with its private services to make your event as memorable as it is seamless
